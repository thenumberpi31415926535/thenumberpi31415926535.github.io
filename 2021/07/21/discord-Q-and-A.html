How to join Pi’s Discord server

Table of Contents

  1. Download Discord app on your phone or PC. If you are using broswer, ensure you have allowed the webiste to use your microphone, if you are not sure you can always download the PC app.
  2. Join Pi’s Discord server: https://discord.gg/vE7QUXGDnS
  3. Create a discord account using your email or phone number.
  4. Verify your email account by checking your email and look for an email from Discord regarding your account creation, check your junk mail if you can not find it in your inbox.
  5. Complete the steps required by Pi’s server.

How to sign up for events?

Table of Contents

  1. Join Pi’s Discord server
  2. Navigate to #annonucements channel under NOTICE BOARD in Discord. Swipe from left to right to get to channel list.
  3. Locate the event you wish to sign up.
  4. Interact to the green tick (✅) at the bottom of each message regarding an event.
  5. Verify if you are listed under attendees.
  6. This will unlock the topic rooms.

Sign up for events on a phone

Troubleshooting:

  1. Should I be concerned with “Un oh, you don’t have webhook permissions in any server you’re in. Ask your server admin to give you the permission or ask them to come follow themselves!”? Not at all, this error message will not affect you from signing up.

Check your audio and mic settings

Table of Contents

  1. Go to your USER SETTINGS and location Voice & Video under APP SETTINGS on your phone or PC. For PC users: Locate USER SETTINGS on the bottom left of your app. This is what your bottom left corner looks like. This is what the settings will look like. For phone users: Swipe from left to right to bring up the channel list, you should locate your user icon on the lower right.
  2. Ensure your INPUT DEVICE and OUTPUT DEVICE are correct.
  3. It’s also a good idea to conduct a MIC TEST using the sensitivity bar while you are here.
  4. INPUT MODE should be set to Voice Activity, this will make it a much more relaxing experience.
  5. Fundamentally, above are the easy steps to follow when it comes to configure your discord setting inside the application. However, it’s possible that the application, such as Discord, does not have permission to use your microphone without your consent.

Whether you are using an App on the phone or PC, it’s important to grant permission for Discord to use your Microphone in order to join our chat. If you are using a browser, I recommend using an app, otherwise, ensure you are using something like Chrome and grant permission for Discord to use your microphone.

How to adjust the volume of a user or a bot in a voice channel

Table of Contents

  1. Locate user in the voice channel that you wish to adjust.
  2. Tap on the user if you are using a phone, right click (ctrl + click on Mac, double tap on trackpad for laptops, etc) when using a PC to bring up menu for the user.
  3. Drag the volume slider to adjust volume.

How to assign roles

Table of Contents

  1. Claim roles by typing command pi#claimroles in any text channel.
  2. Interact with the icons under the message to assign roles to yourself.
  3. You are able to unassign roles via command pi#removeroles
